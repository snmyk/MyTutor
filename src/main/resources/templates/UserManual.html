    • About MyTutorApp

    MyTutor is a web tutor management system designed to automate tutor/TA selection, allocation, monitoring and scheduling. It houses websites for academic courses, which are tailored for each user. If you are a UCT staff member or registered student, you can access the application by logging in using your username and password. Administrators can create new course sites that can be used for viewing the tutor course schedule as well as creating or signing up for tutor activities. Getting started is easy, simply follow the instructions below and start exploring the system.

    Login

    1. Open your internet browser and type http://196.47.239.204:8080/login into the address bar.

    2. Enter your username and password in the respective input boxes.

    3. Click login.

    If you cant login

    1. Click on the forgot password tag that can be found below the login button.

    2. Enter your email and click on submit.

    3. Enter the OTP number sent to the email you provided.

    4. Enter your new password and confirm it.

    5. Click on submit.

    6. You’ll be redirected back to the login page where you can login using your new password.

    Reset Password

    Enter your saved username details and click next. The system will check for your user profile and get the email address saved for it to use it to send the OTP for confirming the user. Enter the OTP and click verify or resend password if you didn't receive your email address; If the OTP is verified you'll be prompted to enter new login password and submit it for changes to be effective. When you done you can click exit which will direct you to the login page and login with your new password.

    Update Profile

    To view profile click on the user icon on the top right of your user homepage. A drop down will show and click on profile details link to view your profile; Your profile details will be shown their after. Click on change details to update them, the updating view will be shown below and then the corresponding button to submit the section you wanted changed. The new details will be shown after clicking submit if you allowed to update them.

    Tutor & TA Registration

    Register a new account (if you're a Tutor or TA)

    1. Open your internet browser and type http://196.47.239.204:8080/login into the address bar.

    2. Click on the register button.

    3. Choose the role (tutor/TA) and enter the required details and click on ‘sign me up’.

    4. Enter the OTP number sent to the email address you provided and click on ‘submit’.

    5. You’ll be redirect to the login page where you can login using your username and password.

    Applications

    Captures Tutor and Ta applications

    Tutor & TA Applications:

    Click on the link provided to you buy your Department administrators to which will direct you to the application form webpage. If you're redirected back to login, enter the link again. An application form will be shown if the application is still open else a message for application is closed will be shown. Enter all the required details. To add course marks, enter the course code and your grade and click on add to add the marks, change the details for the new course marks and click on add to add it. Click submit to submit your application.The link to check your application will be shown and your application ID to track your appliication.

    Check Application:

    Use this link: https://196.47.239.204/applicant/show

    Uploading Documents:

    Click on upload documents select the type and upload your document. If Successful the results will be shown

    Register Successful Applicant:

    On tracking your application if it is Successful you will be allowed to register by click of a button and an email for confirmation and login password will be emailed to you. You can use reset password on the login page to set your application
